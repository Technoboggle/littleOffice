Little Office Booklets

We pray Prime from the Little Office each day, so that is what we are most familiar with.

The files should be available at github.com/veromary/littleOffice.

  • Beata Dei Genetrix, Benedictus
  • Beata Mater, Magnificat
  • Sub Tuum, Nunc Dimittis
  • Regina Caeli - Easter Antiphon - this one to Nunc Dimittis, but same for Benedictus and Magnificat
